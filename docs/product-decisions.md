# Product Decisions

## Work is a proof hub

The Work surface emphasizes evidence of product judgment: the problem, workflow, decisions, operating model, and outcomes rather than a gallery of visual artifacts.

## Historical case studies and current builds are distinct

Historical work explains what was built and learned in context. Current tools demonstrate present-day product thinking. They are related, but they should not imply that a historical case is a live product or that a current build represents a former employer.

## Confidential work is reconstructed safely

Contact Center AI is a sanitized reconstruction. It preserves the truth of product logic, workflow structure, and decision-making while using fictionalized states and synthetic data.

## Product ownership is broader than model ownership

The portfolio evaluates AI products through workflow fit, controls, evaluation, operating readiness, and measurable value. A capable model is one input to product quality, not a substitute for product ownership.

## GA4 consent and Cloudflare analytics have different roles

GA4 is optional and consent-aware. Cloudflare Web Analytics is the native, privacy-first Pages integration. The site keeps both implementations centralized and avoids duplicate beacons or application-managed Cloudflare loaders.

## Featured content is explicit

Article records declare `featured` directly, and the data model enforces exactly one featured article. Homepage and Writing-page prominence is never inferred from array order.
